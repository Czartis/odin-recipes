Homemade mac n cheese

Our recipe for Easy Creamy Mac 'N Cheese has only 5 ingredients and is on the table in less than 30 minutes. What's not to love about that?! Whether you serve it on the side or as a main dish, it's comfort food that the whole family will love.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces elbow macaroni
  • 1 ½ cups milk
  • ¼ cup Country Crock® Spread
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1 ½ cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in large pot according to package directions.
  2. Meanwhile, whisk milk, Country Crock® Spread, and flour in large saucepan.
  3. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, whisking frequently. Reduce heat and cook, stirring occasionally, until thickened, about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in cheese until melted; remove from heat. Drain pasta and fold into the sauce.
